Spotify Toolbox

Spotify Toolbox is a suite of tools that use your Spotify listening habits and playlists to do some super cool things!

Due to the nature of the Spotify API you'll have to authorize each tool individually. You can do so by clicking on the tool you want to use and clicking on the authorize button in the bottom right corner.
